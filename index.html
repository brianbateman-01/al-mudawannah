Mission Statement

Al-Mudawwana Institute is a Sunni initiative devoted to preserving and reviving authentic sacred knowledge upon the way of Ahl al-Sunnah wa-l-Jamāʿah, through sound methodology and qualified guidance. Our aim is to reawaken the spirit of learning and daʿwah, and to build a generation grounded in correct belief, sincere practice, and service to the Ummah.

In less than two months, Allah has gathered around us nearly 260 brothers, and many projects are already underway. Among them are initiatives to strengthen and expand the daʿwah, support Sunni brothers and sisters in need across the world, and the development of an AI system capable of translating classical books—both printed works and manuscripts—in bulk, to make sacred knowledge more accessible.

Central to our mission is establishing a strong and living connection with the Mashāyikh of the East—may Allah preserve them. We seek their knowledge, guidance, and maʿiyyah, so that our efforts remain sincere, balanced, and firmly rooted in the scholarly tradition.
