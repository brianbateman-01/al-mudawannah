Arabic Course

Two tracks: Beginners and Advanced. Seats are limited.

COURSE TRACKS

Beginners: al-ʿArabiyyah Bayna Yadayk Foundations

For brothers and sisters with little to no Arabic background. Step-by-step progression toward a stronger grasp of the language.

Advanced: al-Ājurrūmiyyah Grammar

For students with some foundation who are ready to study classical Arabic grammar and deepen their understanding.

Platform: Telegram • Start: Shortly after Eid • Seats: Limited

Course Notice

This course is designed for brothers and sisters who have little to no background in Arabic and wish to begin their journey from the foundations, progressing step by step toward a stronger understanding of the language. Students are encouraged to remain committed, attend each lesson consistently, and actively engage with the material, as spaces in this class are limited and in high demand.

The teacher humbly requests two things from every student:

  1. Remember him in your du‘ā’.
  2. Give your full attention to the lessons themselves. The objective is not praise or recognition, but that this knowledge sincerely benefits you. From time to time, the teacher may review previous material or ask questions—not to burden you, but to help the knowledge settle firmly and become a lasting benefit, bi’idhnillāh.

Classes are expected to begin shortly after Eid and will be conducted via Telegram. If you have received a message, then you have successfully secured a seat in the class. Please monitor your messages carefully, as further details will be shared soon. Once all spots are filled, an announcement will be made.

A gentle reminder: Seek knowledge with sincerity, patience, and consistency. Even small, steady efforts lead to great openings. Come prepared with a notebook, revise regularly, and do not hesitate to ask questions—true success in learning comes from persistence and humility.
